Frequently Asked Questions

What is your returns policy?

We accept returns for purchased items within the first 7 days after delivery. To be eligible for a return, the item must be completely unused, in its original unaltered packaging, and in the exact same condition that you received it.

How long does delivery take?

We aim to get your order to you as quickly as possible. Please note that our standard delivery processing time is within 3 days after an order is successfully placed.

How are refunds processed?

Once we receive your returned item, our team will inspect it to ensure it perfectly fulfills our return standards (unused and in original packaging). Upon successful verification, we will initiate a refund to your original payment method. Please allow a standard 3-5 business days for the funds to reflect in your account depending on your provider.
