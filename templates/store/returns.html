Returns & Refunds Policy

1. Returns Policy

At Glow More Beauty & Cosmetics, your satisfaction is our priority. Please note that purchased items can be returned within the first 7 days after delivery. To be eligible for a return, the item must be unused, in its original packaging, and in the same condition that you received it.

2. Delivery Information

We strive to get your products to you as quickly as possible. Please be advised that delivery is processed within 3 days after an order is placed.
