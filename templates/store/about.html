About GlowMore

Glow on your own terms.

Our Story

Glow More Beauty & Cosmetics brings you premium skincare, beauty essentials, and lifestyle products designed to help you look and feel your best. Quality products, affordable prices, delivered across Ghana.

We are nestled in the heart of Kasoa-Ofaakor, with an expertly curated collection of high-quality products sourced from world-renowned international brands and talented local artisans. From transformative cosmetics and advanced skincare to haircare and signature fragrances, our team is dedicated to helping you achieve your unique beauty goals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make premium beauty accessible to everyone. We believe quality skincare and beauty essentials should not be a luxury — they should be a daily standard. At GlowMore, we celebrate the diversity and uniqueness of every individual's beauty.
